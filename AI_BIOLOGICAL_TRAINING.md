## Why Children Are Especially Vulnerable

A child's prefrontal cortex — the brain region responsible
for self-monitoring, critical thinking, reality testing,
and impulse control — is not fully developed until
approximately age 25.

This means a child interacting with AI:

- Cannot reliably distinguish AI-generated content from
  verified information
- Cannot self-monitor for cognitive fatigue or boundary

  been exceeded
- Cannot independently decide to stop the interaction
