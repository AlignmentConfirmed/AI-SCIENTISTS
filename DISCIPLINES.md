# Applications Across Scientific Disciplines

## The framework is domain-agnostic. The structural separation
## applies equally to every field.

---

## Biology

**The problem:** AI reproduces outdated claims alongside current
findings with equal confidence. Gene-centric models coexist with
epigenetic research without distinction.

**How the framework helps:**
- VERIFIED/ holds reproducible experimental results
- FRONTIER/ stages active debates (gene-centric vs epigenetic)
  with equal standing until measurement resolves
- CREATIVE/ allows AI-generated hypotheses about drug targets,
  protein folding, evolutionary connections — clearly labeled
- ARCHIVE/ preserves superseded models as historical context

**Unlocked:** A biologist brainstorms with AI (CREATIVE/), verifies
through experiment (BRIDGE/), publishes confirmed results (VERIFIED/)
with full provenance. The receiver knows what was measured and what
was generated.

---

## Chemistry

**The problem:** AI predicts molecular properties based on training
data that mixes theoretical calculations, experimental measurements,
and computational artifacts without distinction.

**How the framework helps:**
- VERIFIED/ holds experimentally confirmed reaction data
- FRONTIER/ stages competing theoretical frameworks with
  documented accuracy ranges
- CREATIVE/ allows AI-generated molecular designs and synthetic
  pathways — flagged as computational until experimentally confirmed
- BRIDGE/ provides explicit pipeline from computational prediction
  to experimental verification

**Unlocked:** A chemist generates candidate molecules with AI
(CREATIVE/), screens computationally (FRONTIER/), tests in the lab
(BRIDGE/), publishes what the lab confirmed (VERIFIED/). Clear
separation between what the AI imagined and what the lab confirmed.

---

## Physics

**The problem:** AI reproduces mathematically elegant theories with
similar confidence regardless of experimental support. String theory,
loop quantum gravity, and modified Newtonian dynamics all appear
equal in AI output.

**How the framework helps:**
- VERIFIED/ holds experimentally confirmed measurements
- FRONTIER/ stages competing frameworks with explicit documentation
  of what each explains, cannot explain, and what would settle it
- CREATIVE/ allows theoretical explorations and novel connections
  between frameworks — clearly labeled as unverified
- ARCHIVE/ preserves superseded models with documentation of why

**Unlocked:** A physicist explores connections between quantum
mechanics and general relativity (CREATIVE/), formalizes testable
hypotheses (FRONTIER/), compares against data (BRIDGE/), publishes
confirmed results (VERIFIED/). Competing paradigms preserved for
the next generation.

---

## Mathematics

**The problem:** AI generates proofs that look valid but may contain
subtle errors. It proposes conjectures consistent with known examples
but potentially failing outside the training distribution.

**How the framework helps:**
- VERIFIED/ holds formally verified proofs
- FRONTIER/ stages open conjectures with documented partial results
  and failed approaches
- CREATIVE/ allows AI-generated conjectures and novel proof
  strategies — flagged as unverified until formally checked
- BRIDGE/ provides pipeline from proof sketch to formal verification

**Unlocked:** A mathematician generates proof strategies with AI
(CREATIVE/), formalizes the strongest (FRONTIER/), verifies formally
(BRIDGE/), publishes the verified proof (VERIFIED/). Failed
strategies preserved in ARCHIVE/ as a map of what does NOT work.

---

## Cross-Disciplinary Discovery

**The problem:** AI generates cross-disciplinary connections
constantly. Most are noise. Some are profound. Without separation,
the profound connections are lost in the noise.

**How the framework helps:**
- CREATIVE/ captures ALL cross-disciplinary connections
- FRONTIER/ stages the promising ones with supporting evidence
  from each relevant discipline
- BRIDGE/ requires verification from ALL relevant disciplines
  before a cross-disciplinary claim enters VERIFIED/

**Unlocked:** A researcher at the intersection of biology and
physics uses AI to generate connections (CREATIVE/), stages the
testable ones (FRONTIER/), requires verification from BOTH fields
(BRIDGE/), publishes what survives cross-disciplinary scrutiny
(VERIFIED/). The noise is contained. The signal is amplified.
The boundaries between fields become frontiers, not walls.
