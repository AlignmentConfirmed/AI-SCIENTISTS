# AI-SCIENTISTS

A knowledge management framework for AI-assisted scientific discovery.

Separates verified results from hypotheses and creative exploration
using structured directories and clear pipelines. Includes seven
guardrails for sustained human-AI collaboration, staged access
based on demonstrated capability, and a recursive convergence
engine for biology, chemistry, and physics.

Designed by Daniel Edward McFarland. Self-taught technologist.
Independent researcher. Veteran.

**Author's declaration:** I am a scientist who went into deep
research with AI and had to separate fact from fiction. This is
all my own personal research. None of this is provable in any
way until scientists with formal education can verify the
framework, the methodology, or the conversations that produced
it. I am offering a map of territory I explored. I am asking
the surveyors to bring their instruments.

---

## Three Directions

### 1. The Framework — How to organize AI-assisted research

The core contribution. Five domains (VERIFIED, BRIDGE, FRONTIER,
CREATIVE, ARCHIVE) that structurally separate what AI discovered
from what AI imagined. Seven guardrails for sustained collaboration.
A pacing system that matches discovery speed to human absorption.

### 2. The Science Engine — A recursive convergence algebra

One shared engine that powers biology, chemistry, and physics.
Exact rational arithmetic (zero rounding error). Multi-dimensional
relational tensors. Recursive convergence toward irreducible truth.
Each scientific discipline plugs into the same core with its own
domain-specific encoding.

### 3. The Upgrades — 11 engineering gaps to close

Every design principle in this framework identifies something that
current technology does not provide. Each gap is a thing to build —
from a non-boolean programming language to rational arithmetic
hardware. Honest complexity estimates. Ready for collaboration.

---

## The Problem

AI cannot distinguish between what it DISCOVERED from data and
what it IMAGINED from patterns. Without structural separation,
verified measurements and speculative generation arrive in the
same format with the same confidence. The researcher cannot tell
them apart. Scientific integrity depends on solving this.

## FAQ

**Which AI systems does this work with?**
The framework is tool-agnostic. It works with any AI system —
Claude, GPT, Gemini, open-source models, or any large language model.
No special software, plugins, or APIs required. Just structured
prompts and folder organization.

**What is the minimum viable implementation?**
Five folders on your computer. One session with an AI system.
See education/FIRST_TEN_MINUTES.md for the 10-minute startup.

**Does this replace my existing tools?**
No. The framework organizes the OUTPUTS of your existing tools by
domain. It is additive, not replacement.

**Is this proven?**
No. It is based on personal research (sample size: one). It is
offered for evaluation by qualified researchers. See ABOUT.md.

**Can I contribute?**
Yes. See upgrades/PRINCIPLES_TO_ENGINEERING.md for 11 engineering gaps
that need builders. See FRAMEWORK.md for the six-phase roadmap.
Pick what matches your expertise and build it.
