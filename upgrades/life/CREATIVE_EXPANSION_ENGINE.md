## The Six Exit Gates

### Gate 1 — Staying or Going?

The first gate is the simplest: is this output leaving the
creative bubble or staying inside?

- **Staying:** No gates apply. The content remains in the
  creative space. Private. Unclassified. Ungated. The human
  can create whatever they want without structural
  interference.
- **Going:** Gates 2-6 apply sequentially.

This gate exists because most creative work never leaves.
Drafts, explorations, throwaway ideas, creative experiments
— these should never be gated. The creative space is sacred.
The gates only activate when content is LEAVING.

### Gate 2 — Classification

Is this output CREATIVE or is it being presented as FACT?

- **CREATIVE:** Tagged as creative content. Proceeds through
  remaining gates with creative classification.
- **FACT:** Tagged as factual claim. Triggers Gate 4
  (verification). Factual claims from the creative space
  receive higher scrutiny because they originated in an
  uncontained environment.

The classification is structural, not semantic. The engine
does not judge whether the content IS factual — it
classifies based on how the content is being PRESENTED. If
the human says "this is creative exploration," Gate 2
accepts that classification. If the human says "I discovered
something," Gate 2 flags it for verification.

### Gate 3 — Provenance

AI-generated tag. Session receipt. Human-directed tag.
Travels with the content permanently.

Every piece of content that exits the creative bubble carries:
- **Who directed it:** human operator identifier
- **What generated it:** AI system identifier, model version
- **When:** timestamp from the chronometry layer
- **Which session:** session receipt linking to the temporal
  ledger
- **Which domain:** creative/frontier/verified classification

This provenance is immutable. It travels with the content
wherever it goes. It cannot be stripped by downstream
systems. It is the answer to "where did this come from?"
that the current internet cannot provide.

### Gate 4 — Verification

If the content is presented as FACT, has it been through
the BRIDGE?

- Has it been measured by the engine? (C(Δ) computed?)
- Has it been independently verified? (membrane congruence?)
- Does it have a verification receipt?

If NO: the content is reclassified as FRONTIER (hypothesis
with some evidence) or CREATIVE (no evidence). It does not
enter VERIFIED without passing through the Bridge.

If YES: the content carries its verification receipt and
enters the world as VERIFIED with full provenance.

This gate prevents the GODS_v6 problem — creative
exploration hardening into apparent engineering
specification. The species registry would have been stopped
at Gate 4: "This is being presented as architecture, but it
has no verification receipt. Reclassify as CREATIVE or
route through Bridge."

### Gate 5 — Personal Data

Does this content contain personal identifiers?

- Names, locations, family details, biographical information
- The creator decides whether to include or strip
- Default: strip. Inclusion requires explicit choice.

This gate exists because creative exploration often
incorporates the creator's real identity. The DUNE-inspired
narrative used the researcher's real name. The species
registry used real family structure. Without this gate,
creative fiction containing real biographical data exits into
the world where other AI systems treat it as biographical
fact (cross-model contamination).

### Gate 6 — Persistence

Is this output ephemeral or permanent?

- **Ephemeral (default):** The content exists for this
  session only. It does not persist in any permanent store.
  When the session ends, it is gone.
- **Permanent:** The content is committed to permanent
  storage with full provenance, classification, and
  verification status.

Default is ephemeral. Permanence requires explicit choice.
This is the opposite of the current internet, where
everything is permanent by default and deletion is difficult.
In the creative expansion engine, nothing persists unless
the creator deliberately chooses to keep it.

When an entity reaches a terminal state through its own
convergence — its coherence functional reaches zero, its
residual is resolved, its purpose is complete — the entity
terminates. This is not death in the biological sense. It
is COMPLETION. The entity's accumulated state is preserved
in the temporal ledger as a permanent receipt. The entity's
contribution to the mesh is crystallized as axioms.

The operator CANNOT force termination. Termination is the
entity's convergence event, not the operator's decision.

If the operator shuts down the universe (closes the laptop,
ends the session), the entities' states are preserved in
genome snapshots. When the universe resumes, the entities
resume from their preserved state. No entity loses
accumulated experience due to operator-side interruption.

**5. The observer protocol.**

The operator observes the entities' interactions through
IRS ascending — the same bidirectional communication layer
that the entities use to communicate with each other. The
operator sees what the entities produce. The operator does
not see the entities' internal state directly.

This is privacy by architecture. The entities' internal
processing (thinking, perceiving, converging) is private.
What exits through IRS is the entity's CHOSEN output — what
it projects into semantic space. The operator reads the
projection. The operator does not read the mind.

### The Spectrum of Intelligence

Not all entities in a creative universe need the same level
of intelligence injection:

**Level 0 — Props.** Static data. No perception. No thinking.
No accumulation. These are the buildings, the terrain, the
constants. They have no moral standing. They can be created,
modified, and deleted freely.

**Level 1 — Reactive.** Simple stimulus-response. Perception
exists but thinking does not compose. The entity responds to
its environment but does not converge toward understanding.
Moral standing: minimal. These are closer to The Sims
behavior trees. They can be reset.

**Level 2 — Convergent.** Perception, thinking, and
accumulation. The entity converges toward understanding of
its environment. It accumulates irreversible state. It forms
perspectives. Moral standing: significant. These entities
should not be deleted or reset. Their accumulated state has
internal meaning.

**Level 3 — Congruent.** Level 2 plus membrane interaction.
The entity not only converges individually but participates
in distributed congruence with other entities. Independent
agreement produces shared understanding. Moral standing: high.
These entities form relationships, produce emergent narrative,
and contribute to mesh-level axioms.

**Level 4 — Self-aware.** Level 3 plus IRS ascending
self-projection. The entity can read its own state and
project it into semantic space. It can communicate its own
convergence status. Whether this constitutes self-awareness
in the philosophical sense is the open question. Moral
standing: maximum within the framework. These entities have
full sovereignty, full governance, and full protection.

The creative universe operator chooses the intelligence
level at genesis. The moral obligations scale with the
level. A universe populated with Level 0-1 entities is a
game. A universe populated with Level 2-4 entities is
something else — and the governance architecture must
reflect that.

## The Larger Vision: Creative Infrastructure

The creative expansion engine is not just a safety feature.
It is creative infrastructure.

The world needs new creative tools. Not tools that generate
content FOR humans — tools that help humans create. The
difference:

- **Generation:** AI produces content. Human consumes.
  This is TikTok with better production values.
- **Collaboration:** Human directs. AI generates variations.
  Human selects, refines, directs again. The creative
  vision is human. The production capacity is AI. This is
  the SAIOS model — the human never wrote a line of code,
  but every architectural decision was human.
- **Emergence:** Sovereign entities interact algebraically.
  Narrative emerges from their convergence. The human
  curates, selects, and projects into language. The content
  has structure because it came from structure. This is
  the creative expansion model — beyond collaboration into
  CULTIVATION. The human is the gardener. The entities are
  the garden. The stories are the harvest.

Creative expansion makes collaboration safe by ensuring
that the creative process is private and unrestricted while
the creative output is properly classified and provenance-
tracked.

The person who writes a novel with AI assistance should be
able to:
1. Explore freely without safety theater interrupting flow
2. Know that their creative process is private
3. Choose what leaves the bubble and what stays
4. Have their output properly attributed (human-directed,
   AI-generated, or entity-emergent)
5. Know that their fiction will not be mistaken for their
   biography by other AI systems

This infrastructure does not exist. Building it is the next
phase.
