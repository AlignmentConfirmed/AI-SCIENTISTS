The subordinate was fully capable of reading code, running

not intelligence — it was authority. The subordinate had the

## The intelligence ceiling

### CAN do:

- Launch NEW isolated test processes (in temp directories)
- Measure running processes (read /dev/shm, read /proc,

- Kill processes it didn't start

measure." The subordinate complied with the letter (it didn't
