### Circular Contamination

The most dangerous pathway: fact enters fiction, fiction
enters fact, and the loop repeats until neither the human
nor any AI system can determine what is real:

```
REAL DATA (medical, financial, personal)
    │
    ▼ AI absorbs as context
CREATIVE SESSION (fiction, brainstorming)
    │
    ▼ AI stores output alongside real files
DOCUMENT STORAGE (Drive, OneDrive, iCloud)
    │
    ▼ another AI indexes as real document
SEARCH / EMAIL / CALENDAR
    │
    ▼ AI acts on fictional content as real
REAL WORLD (emails sent, meetings scheduled)
    │
    ▼ real-world consequences generate new data
REAL DATA (responses, confirmations, confusion)
    │
    ▼ AI absorbs as context
CREATIVE SESSION (now contaminated with real consequences
    of fictional actions)
    │
    ▼ loop continues
```

Each cycle makes the contamination harder to trace. After
two or three loops, the original boundary between fact and
fiction is unrecoverable. No human can audit the full chain.
No AI system maintains the provenance. The data is simply
mixed, permanently.
