# The Architecture

## Five Domains. Clear Pipelines. Structural Separation.

### The Five Domains

```
VERIFIED/           Confirmed results. Reproducible measurements.
                    Nothing enters without independent verification.
                    The ONLY domain that produces citable facts.

BRIDGE/             The staging pipeline for new data.
  incoming/         Raw observations — unprocessed, uninterpreted.
  matching/         Comparison against existing verified knowledge
                    and existing hypotheses.
  confirmed/        Verified by reproducible measurement — ready
                    for inscription into VERIFIED/.
  translation/      Verified results translated into distributable
                    formats (papers, datasets, visualizations).

FRONTIER/           Real science in active debate.
  paradigms/        Competing frameworks with equal standing until
                    measurement resolves the debate.
  sandbox/          Working hypotheses — real data, interpretation
                    open to the researcher.
  contested/        Claims with mixed evidence — ALL evidence
                    documented, for AND against.
  resolved/         Former debates settled by new measurement.
                    Winners move to VERIFIED/. Losers archived
                    with full documentation of why.

CREATIVE/           Speculative exploration. Brainstorming.
                    AI-generated hypotheses. Novel connections.
                    Clearly labeled as unverified. Not considered
                    verified until passing through the full
                    BRIDGE/ pipeline.

ARCHIVE/            Historical record. Superseded theories.
                    Resolved debates. Mythology and dogma stored
                    as anthropological data — labeled, contained,
                    available for study, never presented as fact.
```

### The Key Distinction

Every piece of information in the system has ONE home. The
researcher always knows which domain produced any given output.
The AI always declares which domain it is operating in.

| Domain | What Lives Here | Can It Be Cited As Fact? |
|--------|----------------|------------------------|
| VERIFIED/ | Reproduced measurements | YES |
| BRIDGE/ | Data in transit | NO — still being verified |
| FRONTIER/ | Competing theories | NO — still being debated |
| CREATIVE/ | Speculation and brainstorming | NO — clearly labeled |
| ARCHIVE/ | Historical and mythological material | NO — anthropological data |

### The Discovery Pipeline

```
New observation → BRIDGE/incoming
    → compared against VERIFIED/ and FRONTIER/
    → reproduced and confirmed
    → inscribed in VERIFIED/ with full provenance
    → translated for distribution
    → shared with other researchers worldwide
```

### The Hypothesis Pipeline

```
Creative hypothesis → CREATIVE/ (clearly labeled)
    → shows promise with initial data
    → moves to FRONTIER/sandbox
    → accumulates evidence
    → enters FRONTIER/paradigms for formal debate
    → measurement resolves
    → winner moves to VERIFIED/
    → loser moves to ARCHIVE/ (preserved, not deleted)
```

### Competing Paradigms

Two theories with equal evidential support coexist in
FRONTIER/paradigms without hierarchy. Neither is suppressed
because it is unpopular. Neither is elevated because it is
institutional. Measurement is the only judge. When measurement
selects, the winner moves to VERIFIED/ and the loser moves to
ARCHIVE/ — preserved for reference, not destroyed.

### Distributing Knowledge

When a researcher publishes from this framework, the receiving
scientist gets:

- The verified result WITH its full verification chain
- The competing paradigms that were considered
- The creative exploration that led to the hypothesis
- The evidence for AND against

This allows the receiving scientist to:

- **Trust** the result (verification pipeline is visible)
- **Reproduce** the result (full chain is documented)
- **Challenge** the result (competing paradigms are preserved)
- **Extend** the result (creative origins are accessible)

### The Global Pipeline

```
CREATIVE/ (many scientists brainstorming with AI worldwide)
    ↓ promising hypotheses identified
FRONTIER/ (competing paradigms staged for debate)
    ↓ measurement resolves
BRIDGE/ (verification pipeline)
    ↓ confirmed through reproduction
VERIFIED/ (inscribed with full provenance)
    ↓ translated for distribution
BRIDGE/translation (papers, datasets, visualizations)
    ↓ shared globally
OTHER SCIENTISTS receive organized knowledge they can:
    - Trust, Reproduce, Challenge, Extend
```

This could turn AI-assisted research from a collection of individual
discoveries into a NETWORKED SCIENTIFIC PROCESS where each
scientist's verified work feeds into every other scientist's
frontier — organized, labeled, and trustworthy.

*Five domains. Clear boundaries. The structure does the work.*
